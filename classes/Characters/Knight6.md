Class Feature | Explanation
------------- | -----------
Code of Conduct | A Knight must refuse bonuses from Aid Another actions in combat, a Knight must refrain from the use of poisons of any kind, and a Knight may not voluntarily change shape.
Draw Fire | You may use a swift action to force a creature with 10 or less Intelligence to attack you. That creature must make a will save (DC 10 + 1/2 Knight level + constitution modifier) or spend all of its actions moving towards or attacking you. 
Shield Mastery | Subtract 6 from the armor check penalty given by your shield.
Bastion of Defense | All adjacent allies gain a +2 dodge bonus to AC and Reflex save (this does not include you).
Defensive Fighter | You only get a -2 penalty on attacks for fighting defensively. 
Grounded | Add 1d6 to the damage of any attack you make during a turn in which you take no movement apart from a 5 foot step.
Energy Resistance | Gain energy resistance to any energy type equal to your shield bonus. You can spend a swift action to change the energy type.
Speak to Animals | Add your Knight level to your Ride and Handle Animal checks.
Damage Reduction | You have damage reduction X/- where X is your shield bonus. This can only come from physically held shields, magical enhancements to shield bonus do not increase this.
Immunity to Fear | You are immune to Fear effects.
Knightly Spirit | You can spend a move action to recover any attribute damage or drain.
Sacrifice | You may spend an immediate action to make yourself the target of an attack or target effect towards any adjacent ally.
Mettle | If you succeed on a Fortitude or Will save suffer no negative effects.
Defend Others | Allies adjacent to you gain Evasion (you do not).
Immovable Stance | You can spend a free action to enter Immovable Stance. You gain +4 Strength, +4 Constitution, +4 bonus on all saves, +4 to AC, and +2 to DR. While Immovable Stance is active you can take no movement besides 5 foot steps. After ending Immovable Stance you cannot reactivate it until you've rested for 1 minute.
Quick Recovery | If you are stunned, dazed, or paralyzed, that condition ends at the end of your turn.

Dwarves can also sense approximate depth underground.
